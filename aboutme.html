INSTA

"My path began at Budai Art School, where I first learned the foundations of metalsmithing and later the precision of goldsmithing."

"Building on that craftsmanship, my studies at MOME as a designer-maker opened new perspectives, giving me space to experiment with materials, forms, and techniques."

"Today I see myself as an experimental maker, using objects as a way to express how I perceive the world."
